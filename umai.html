ENGINE UNREAL 5
ROLE LEAD DEV
DURATION 2 MONTHS
TEAM 2 PROG • 3 ART • 2 GD

MISSION BRIEFING

UMAI! is a 4-player 3D Couch Game where you play as eccentric sushi characters fighting for the ultimate honor: to be chosen and devoured by the customers!

Objective: Fish for ingredients, craft powerful makis, sink your rivals' ships, and prove you are the most delicious dish on the menu!

PLAY ON ITCH.IO
GAMEPLAY OVERVIEW

TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION

  • > Interaction Engine: Implementation of the ICouchInteractable interface to standardize interactions (Pickable/Interactable). Creation of UCouchMovement using Splines Components and Timelines to manage dynamic obstacles and conveyor belts parametrically.
  • > Custom Physics Projectile: UCouchProjectile uses a deterministic physics simulation (Gravity Z: -980.0) independent of PhysX to ensure responsive and synchronized gameplay.
  • > Game Architecture: Usage of a GameInstanceSubsystem (UCouchGameManagerSubSystem) as a persistent State Machine to manage Rounds, the Global Timer, and Win Conditions.
INTERACTION SYSTEM

FISHING MECHANICS 2.0

  • > QTE System: Complex mechanic using UCableComponent for line physics. Conflict management via a QTE state (StartQte / RewindQte) where players must "mash" buttons to win the contested object.
  • > Feedback: Juice system driven by BlueprintNativeEvents allowing designers to iterate on Game Feel (Camera Shake, Force Feedback) without recompilation.
FISHING & QTE

CATAPULT & DEFENSE

  • > Ballistics: Real-time parabolic trajectory calculation based on Velocity and a Power Curve (CurveShoot).
  • > Weapon Logic: Class ACouchCatapult inheriting from ACouchInteractableWeapons. Manual reloading system (Reload) requiring physical ammo (ACouchStaticCanonBall).
CATAPULT LOGIC

TOOLS & PLUGINS

Development of a Level Selection Plugin: An Editor Utility Widget tool to organize, categorize, and instantly load development and production maps, optimizing the entire team's workflow.
